9M2IBR

LoRa APRS Tracker − Web Flasher

First flash or factory reset − writes bootloader, partitions and filesystem together (web_factory.bin). Existing SPIFFS config will be erased.

Disclaimer: flashing firmware is done at your own risk. Double-check you selected the correct board variant before flashing − an incorrect selection can leave the device unresponsive. The maintainer is not responsible for damage, data loss, or bricked hardware resulting from use of this tool.
Your browser does not support installing firmware on ESP devices. Use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.
This page must be served over HTTPS to flash a device.
